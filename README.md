## ✨ About BloomBuhay

BloomBuhay is a **PWA app prototype** that helps Filipinas navigate motherhood – from trying to conceive, through pregnancy, and into postpartum. Think of it as your **Ate BB** (big sister) in your pocket: she listens like a sister, thinks like a doctor, and acts like a 24/7 lifeline.

**Current features (MVP prototype):**
- 💬 **Ate BB** – Mock AI chat that answers common pregnancy questions
- 📚 **Gabay** – Feed of verified articles & videos from partner clinics
- 🏥 **Konsulta** – Find nearby clinics, book appointments, PhilHealth guide
- 📊 **3‑Stage Metrics** – Track health for Trying → Pregnant → Postpartum

> **Note:** This is a **prototype** – Ate BB responds to pre‑set questions only. The full version will use live AI.
